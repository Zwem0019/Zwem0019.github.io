Blog 2 - Study choice

Motivation

I came to this study after a long search for what I like. The interest in computers has been around for a long time. In high school I kind of lost sight of it. Until I had to program at my internship company. I liked that and it also stimulated my old love for computers. I looked at Avans Hogeschool, but that wasn't what I was looking for. Then I came to HZ and this study appealed to me.

What I did to come to this conclusion

The thing I looked at was a open day at TEAM academy in Amsterdam, that was a study about entrepreneurship. That study did not totally fit me and it was expensive. So that option was gone. Then I had to program for my internship company. I liked that so I began searching for computer study's. I went to an open day at Avans Hogeschool for the study Computer science, but I did'nt like the study and the distance I had to travel. So HZ was my next choice and this study clicked with me.

What I want to do after this study

Ultimately I want to start my own company, but maybe I will work at a other company first to get some experience.
