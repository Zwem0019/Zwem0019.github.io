FAQ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?


Go to print.hz.nl and login with your schoolaccount.
Next select a document and a printer. The document will be printed.

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?


Go to a printer in the schoolbuilding. Scan your HZ pass, lay your document on the scanarea of the machine and press scan. The scan will be sent to your HZ-mail.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?


Go to webshop.hz.nl and search for the product(s) you want. Add them to your cart. Once everthing is added, go to the cart. Click on Pay and select a method and follow further instructions.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?


Click Here to find the latest instructions on how to handle with COVID.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?


Click Here to find the rooms planner. Make sure to reserve at the right location. Rooms with the prefix GW are on the location Middelburg. All other prefixes are on the location Vlissingen.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?


Free parking is available at the parking of PZEM , Poelendaelesingel 8 , Middelburg. Drive upto barrier and scan your HZ-pass. The barrier will open and you can park your car.
